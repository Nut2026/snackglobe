## Vision & Mission

**Vision**: A world where cultural heritage is preserved and shared through the universal language of food.

**Mission**: To build cultural infrastructure for the connected future by using AI to tell the stories behind traditional snacks and making them accessible to everyone via customisable recipes.

---

## About SnackGlobe

SnackGlobe is a cultural exploration platform where snacks serve as windows to understanding the world. It allows users to:

- **Discover** traditional snacks from around the world through an interactive map
- **Experience** immersive cultural stories from characters with voice synthesis
- **Craft** personalised recipes adapted to dietary needs and preferences
- **Save** customised recipes to a personal collection (the "Shack")

**Why this matters**: Traditional recipes are disappearing at alarming rates. A generation grows up not knowing the stories behind what they eat. SnackGlobe preserves these stories for the future.

---

## Features

### Interactive Map Discovery
- "Set Sail" to discover — search for traditional snacks and beverages, filter between regions and countries, or let the wind take you!
- Panel reveals cultural authenticity, hidden nutrients, and recipe

### Immersive Cultural Stories
- First-person narratives from characters who treasure the snack and beverage
- Voice synthesis to hear the stories out loud
- Characters include: bakers, tea masters, confectioners, survivors

### Personalised Recipe Crafting
- Original and Customised recipe tabs
- Dietary filters: Low Sugar, Vegan, Gluten-Free, High Protein
- Natural language customisation: "I'm missing coconut milk"
- Cuisine fusion from up to 8 regions
- Transform recipes to fit your needs while preserving cultural authenticity

### The Shack (Personal Collection)
- Save customised recipes
- Star favourites
- Mark snacks as "Crafted" with count tracking
- Remove with undo functionality
